🐾 My Favorite Animal: The Dolphin 🐬

Dolphins are smart and friendly sea animals. They live in oceans all over the world. Dolphins love to play, jump, and swim in groups called pods. They can talk to each other using clicks and whistles!

🌟 Fun Facts About Dolphins

1. Dolphins can swim up to 25 miles per hour!

2. They sleep with one eye open to stay safe.

3. Dolphins are super playful and love to surf on waves!

❤️ Why I Like Dolphins

I like dolphins because they are kind and intelligent. They always look happy and they swim so fast! I want to see one in real life one day.
