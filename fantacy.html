Once upon a time, in a magical forest filled with glowing trees and talking animals, a brave little explorer named Mia discovered a secret path. The path led her to a sparkling river guarded by a wise old owl. The owl told Mia that whoever could solve his riddle would unlock the rainbow bridge to the Land of Sweets!

Mia thought hard, answered the riddle, and suddenly the bridge appeared! She skipped across and found lollipop trees, chocolate rivers, and candy houses. It was the sweetest adventure ever!
