What Are Tigers?

Tigers are the biggest wild cats in the world. They are strong, fast, and very smart. Tigers have beautiful orange fur with black stripes and a long tail.

They are great hunters and like to live in forests, jungles, and grasslands.

Interesting Facts

  • Tigers can swim very well.
  • They can roar very loudly!
  • Each tiger has a unique stripe pattern.
  • Tigers sleep up to 16 hours a day.

Where Do They Live?

Tigers live in countries like India, China, Indonesia, and Russia. They love places with water, lots of trees, and plenty of space to hunt.

What Do They Eat?

Tigers are carnivores. This means they eat meat. Their favorite foods are:

  • Deer
  • Buffalo
  • Wild boar
  • Sometimes fish

Types of Tigers

There are different types of tigers in the world. Here are some of the most popular ones:

  • Bengal Tiger
  • Siberian Tiger
  • Indochinese Tiger
  • Malayan Tiger
  • South China Tiger
  • Sumatran Tiger
